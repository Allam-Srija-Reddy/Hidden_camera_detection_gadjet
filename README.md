# Hidden_camera_detection_gadjet
She Shield – Hidden Camera Detector A portable, non-invasive device that detects hidden cameras in real time by identifying the characteristic IR reflection and lens glint signatures they emit — designed to protect personal privacy in hotels, changing rooms, and public spaces.
She Shield – 360° Hidden Camera Detector
She Shield is a privacy protection device that uses a circular array of 12 IR LEDs arranged in a uniform pitch around a central photodiode sensor, enabling omnidirectional detection of hidden cameras. When IR light emitted by the LEDs strikes a camera lens, the retro-reflective coating on the lens bounces the light back toward the sensor — this unique signature is detected and flagged as a potential threat. The circular LED layout ensures no blind spots, covering a full 360° sweep in a single scan, making it effective in hotel rooms, trial rooms, and public washrooms. Built around the ESP32 microcontroller, the system processes the reflected signal in real time and alerts the user via buzzer and LED indicators, offering a compact, low-cost, and highly reliable solution for everyday personal safety.
